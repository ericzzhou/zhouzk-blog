How to Enjoy MARUKYU KOYAMAEN

From traditional tea ceremony to modern desserts — this matcha elevates everything it touches.

Pure Matcha (Usucha)

Whisk 2g with 70ml of 80°C water for a perfect bowl of thin matcha. The emerald green crema and rich umami speak for themselves.

Matcha Latte

Sift matcha into warm milk for a café-quality latte at home. The mild bitterness and fresh astringent pair beautifully with oat or whole milk.
